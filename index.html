About me

I'm a data scientist focused on applied AI — the part of the field where embeddings, LLMs, and agents actually have to work on noisy, inconsistent, real-world data. Most of my recent work has been building entity-resolution and reasoning systems at the million-record scale: vector search to find candidates, clustering to group them, and LLMs (with a healthy dose of agentic validation) to make the final call.

Underneath that is a strong analytics foundation — SQL pipelines, Tableau and Power BI dashboards, statistical modeling, classic ML. I think the best AI work comes from people who know what the data actually looks like before they hand it to a model, and I write about both sides of that craft on Medium.

LLMs & Agentic AI

LLM-as-Judge pipelines, multi-agent workflows (Generator / Critic / Improver / Memory), Cohere & Command-family models, RAG with vector DBs.

Embeddings & Vector Search

Semantic matching, multilingual embeddings, DBSCAN clustering with token blocking, scalable similarity search at 1M+ records.

Analytics & ML

Classification, regression, segmentation; SQL & ETL pipelines; Tableau and Power BI dashboards that decision-makers actually use.
